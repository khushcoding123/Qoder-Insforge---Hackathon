About

TradeMentor AI is a chart-side educational trading coach. It uses Claude's AI to help you think through chart setups using the Socratic method — asking guiding questions rather than giving signals.

Chart Vision: Click "Analyze" in the panel to capture your chart and have AI describe what it sees, then guide you through it.

This extension is for educational purposes only and does not provide financial advice or trading signals.
